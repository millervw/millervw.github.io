About Me

Business Intelligence Developer specializing in Dynamics 365 F&O / CE reporting solutions. Skilled in designing, developing, and deploying analytics solutions to deliver actionable insights, support data-driven decision making, and optimize business processes.

  • Cross-functional skills in business intelligence, data analysis, data engineering, development, and system administration
  • Architect end-to-end solutions built on Microsoft Fabric and / or Azure Synapse, utilizing Data Factory, Data Engineering, Lakehouse, and Data Warehouse components
  • Expert in Power BI: ETL, modeling, visualization, KPI tracking, dashboard creation, statistical analysis, paginated reports, administration, and governance
  • Extensive experience designing and implementing reporting and analytics solutions for Microsoft Dynamics 365 Finance & Operations and Customer Engagement, leveraging tools such as Fabric, Azure Synapse Link, Data Management Framework (DMF), Data Entities, OData, and Power Automate
  • Background in SQL Server, Azure SQL, Dataverse, and Oracle as data sources for reporting
  • Build trust in data and report accuracy through data validation techniques and reconciliation processes
  • Combine foundational business intelligence (KPIs, dashboards, summary reports, etc.) with deep dives into the data and statistical analysis to discover additional insights
  • Drive user adoption through training, technical support, and documentation
  • Manage systems implementation processes, including requirements definition, design, development, integration, deployment, and maintenance
